Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you deliver outside Kano?

Yes! While our factory is in Kano, we deliver nationwide to states like Lagos, Abuja, Kaduna, and Jigawa. Delivery fees depend on the quantity and location.

Is your Texcote paint washable?

Our Texcote is designed for exterior durability and is weather-resistant. For interior washable walls, we recommend our Royal Satin or Silk Sheen products.

Can I request a custom color?

Absolutely. We have a color mixing lab and can match any color you bring to us. Just send us a sample or a picture via WhatsApp.

Do you offer painting services or just sales?

We do both! We can supply the paint, and we also have a team of expert painters to handle your project from start to finish with a guarantee of quality.
